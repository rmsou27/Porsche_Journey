The Founder:Ferdinand Porsche

Ferdinand Porsche was a German automotive engineer and founder of the Porsche AG. He is best known for creating the first gasoline electric hybrid vehicle, the Volkswagen Beetle, the Auto Union racing cars, the Mercedes-Benz SS/SSK, and several other important developments and Porsche automobiles.
